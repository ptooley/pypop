Welcome to PyPOP’s documentation!¶

The PyPOP package is designed to make it easy to perform application performance analyses based on the POP methodology. The primary goals of PyPOP are:

  • Easy calculation of POP metrics

  • High quality figure generation

  • Easy access to underlying data and statistics (using Pandas)

  • Flexible and extensible design
